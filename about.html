Our story

NeonPlay started as a simple wish: a place where families, friends and after-work crews could walk in, light up and just have fun together. No fiddly coins. No awkward queues. Just bright lights, your play card and a wall of cabinets to choose from.

Today we're proud to be the brightest spot on the highstreet — a venue locals call their own, host birthdays in, and bring out-of-town visitors to show off.

What you'll find

Over 50 hand-picked games across racing, sports, rhythm, shooters and classic prize cabinets. Everything runs on our tap-to-play card system — top up once, play all day.

We come to the venue to play together — that's the whole point. We don't run online games, and we'll never ask you to. Real cabinets, real lights, real high-fives.
