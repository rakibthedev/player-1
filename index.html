How it works

From the moment you walk in to the moment you leave — here's what your Player 1 experience looks like.

01
Walk in & get your card

Grab a play card at the front desk. Load it with credits — the more you load, the more you save.

02
Tap & play anything

Tap your card on any game reader. No queues, no coins. Just instant action on 50+ machines.

03
Earn tickets & win

Score big, collect digital tickets, and redeem them for prizes at our prize wall. Take on the leaderboard.

04
Top up & come back

Credits never expire. Top up any time — at the desk or via our app — and your card remembers you.
